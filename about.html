About us

Many of Americans are burdened by high amounts of debt. If your quality of life is being affected by your debt situation and if you're thinking about Debt Elimination, Credit Repair or even Bankruptcy, let the experts at High Mark Legal Services evaluate your claim.

We will help you come up with a plan to work with your creditors and if applicable help you eliminate “junk debt” that is not properly validated through our debt elimination program. If you later decide that you want to proceed with Bankruptcy or if you simply feel that you need a “fresh start” to get your life back on track we can help you rebuilt your financial future through our years of experience in every step of the way.

We know the credit and collections industry very well. We know its cycles, its internal policies and procedures. To make the best of your financial situation now and in the future, it’s important you find a company with the right people with experience to successfully represent you - a group who understands the difficulties and challenges you're currently facing.
