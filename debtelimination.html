Debt Elimination

Are you ready to eliminate your debts and become financially free?  Would you like to stop debt collectors from harassing you and turn the tables on them for a change?

Most debt elimination companies use a defensive strategy which is ineffective.

At the offices of High Mark Legal Services we take a different approach.   Using Federal laws such as the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) and the Federal Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA); to put the law on your side and make debt collectors legally validate the debt and prove their legal authority to collect from you. These laws were designed to protect the consumer’s rights when they engage in debt collection and also to insure that creditors report correct information regarding your accounts to the credit reporting agencies accurately.

We have a proven system to help you fight back which disputes incorrect, old or inaccurate information. Our procedure disputes the very account that debt collectors are attempting to collect.  We ask the debt collector for proof and documentation to validate the debt they allege you owe them. We require them to provide validation and proof of their legal authority to collect from you.  You’d be surprised how often they simply can’t because of their lack of documentation. We then ask them to show that they have legal authority to collect from you. We investigate potential violations of the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA) and the Federal Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA).

Of course it’s not possible to guarantee these strategies one hundred percent, but we have helped many consumers beat debt collectors using this strategy. Debt Elimination is not for everyone and every situation is unique. However, we encourage you to find out more information to see if you qualify contact us to discuss your options.

The attorneys are betting the odds that you won’t respond to their letters or show up in court to fight.  They’re looking for easy money.  We’re betting they’ll have a hard time proving the validity of the debt or wanting to spend their time and resources to do so.  We’re looking to, whenever possible, eliminate your debt!
